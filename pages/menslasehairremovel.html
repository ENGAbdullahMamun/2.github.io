BBL Laser Hair Removal

Description

At Prime we have the best and latest, pain-free laser hair removal system. Introducing BBL Laser Hair Removal also referred to as the Forever Bare BBL to help you achieve permanent hair reduction. Depending on the treatment area, a session of laser hair removal can be as little as a few minutes, and up to an hour or more. After each session, the hair in the treated area will appear finer and lighter. Packages are available for up to 20% savings and we offer a multiple area discount on top of that to save you even more.

Before and After Your Treatment Optimal results for your hair removal sessions can be achieved by following a few important steps:

Be sure to shave the area to be treated the night before your session. Due to the difficulty of achieving a clean shave in the spa please shave in the comfort of your own home. Do not tan for four weeks prior to laser treatments and refrain from using self tanning products for two weeks. Because the success of laser hair removal relies on the destruction of the pigmented hair follicle, do not pluck, wax, or have electrolysis for three weeks prior to treatment. Retinol, Renova, Retin-A, glycolic acids, bleaching creams and exfoliants should be stopped three to four days prior to treatment. Pricing depends on treatment area, package pricing available.
